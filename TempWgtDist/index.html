Unit Conversion
Temperature


To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula the `C` with your temperature in celsius: (C * 9/5) + 32




To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in kilograms: kg * 2.205
Distance



To convert kilometers to mile yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilometers: km ÷ 1.609
